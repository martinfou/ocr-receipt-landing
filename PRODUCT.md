# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Desktop power users, developers, small business owners, and privacy-conscious professionals who need to extract structured data (vendor, date, amount, items) from PDF/image receipts securely. They want an offline-first solution that runs locally on Mac, Windows, and Linux without uploading sensitive financial data to the cloud or paying recurring subscriptions.

## Product Purpose
Provide a highly secure, private, and lightning-fast local receipt OCR scanning tool. Success means showcasing this privacy-first, cloudless utility with an engaging, interactive, and premium landing page that immediately visualizes how scanning works locally.

## Brand Personality
Technical & Premium. Sleek, privacy-first, expert-level utility, and high-performance. Design elements feel precise, fast, and secure.

## Anti-references
- SaaS Cliché: Warm AI-beige/cream/sand backgrounds, side-stripe cards, gradient text, cheap illustrative doodles, and the hero-metric template (big numbers, gradient eyebrows, identical cards).
- Overstimulating design: Rainbow gradient trails, excessive bounce transitions, or busy visual particles.

## Design Principles
1. Privacy is Tangible: Showcase local-first processing clearly using visual, interactive, or animated simulations of processing file data entirely on the user's device.
2. Premium Precision: Utilize fine borders, dark-mode surfaces, subtle glows, and monospace type details to convey native desktop performance and high accuracy.
3. Restrained Sophistication: Rely on dark backgrounds with neutral tones and clean primary accents (brand-blue, scan-glows) instead of warm sand tones or loud gradients.
4. Seamless Flow: Animate micro-interactions (e.g., interactive scanning highlights, smooth dropdowns, and button hovers) to make the experience feel alive and premium.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast: WCAG AAA/AA compliance with strong contrast for body text and placeholders against dark surfaces.
- Motion: Honoring `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` on all custom scanning and interface animations.
